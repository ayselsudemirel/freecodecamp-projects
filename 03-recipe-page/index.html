Chocolate Pancakes

These chocolate pancakes are soft, sweet, and perfect for a delicious breakfast or dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, cocoa powder, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add the milk and egg, then stir until the batter is smooth.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat and pour in a small amount of batter.
  4. Cook each pancake for a few minutes on both sides.
  5. Serve warm with fruit, chocolate sauce, or honey.
